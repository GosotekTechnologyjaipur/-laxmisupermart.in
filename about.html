About Laxi Super Mart

Good Products. Great Value. Since 2009.

Our Story

Laxi Super Mart was founded in 2009 in Jaipur, Rajasthan with a simple belief — every Indian family deserves access to quality products at fair prices. Starting as a single 5,000 sq ft store in C-Scheme, we focused on what matters most: keeping costs low and passing every saving to our customers.

Unlike traditional retail, we own all our stores, work directly with manufacturers, and operate on an Everyday Low Cost — Everyday Low Price (EDLC-EDLP) model. Today, with 85+ stores across 12 states, we remain committed to our founding promise: no gimmicks, no MRP selling — just honest prices, every day.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from sourcing to shelf.

Quality First

Every product on our shelves passes strict quality checks. We partner directly with trusted brands and verified suppliers to ensure freshness and authenticity.

Everyday Low Prices

No fake discounts or inflated MRPs. Our EDLC-EDLP model means you get the lowest possible price every day — not just during sales.

Local Sourcing

We source fresh produce from local farmers and regional suppliers, reducing transit time and supporting local agriculture across India.

Community First

Each Laxi Mart store creates 50-60 local jobs. We invest in the communities we serve through employment, fair wages, and accessible pricing.

Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped Laxi Super Mart over the years.

2009
Founded in Jaipur, Rajasthan — our first 5,000 sq ft value retail store in C-Scheme.
2012
Expanded to 10 stores across Rajasthan, establishing our EDLC-EDLP model as a proven success.
2015
Entered Gujarat and Maharashtra, bringing everyday low prices to new markets.
2018
Launched online ordering, extending the Laxi Mart experience to customers who prefer doorstep delivery.
2021
Crossed 50 stores milestone, serving millions of value-conscious families across India.
2024
85+ stores across 12 states with 5,000+ employees, serving 2.5 million customers every month.
