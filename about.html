Our Story

From a single store in Jaipur to 85+ stores across India

Laxi Super Mart was founded in 2009 in Jaipur, Rajasthan with a simple belief — every Indian family deserves access to quality products at fair prices. Starting as a single 5,000 sq ft store in C-Scheme, we focused on what matters most: keeping costs low and passing every saving to our customers.

"No gimmicks, no inflated MRPs — just honest prices, every single day. That's the promise we started with, and that's the promise we keep."

Unlike traditional retail, we own all our stores, work directly with manufacturers, and operate on an Everyday Low Cost — Everyday Low Price (EDLC-EDLP) model. We don't offer franchises. Every store is company-owned and company-operated, ensuring consistent quality and pricing across all locations.

What we stand for

The principles that guide every decision — from sourcing to shelf.

Quality First

Every product passes strict quality checks. We partner directly with trusted brands and verified suppliers to ensure freshness and authenticity.

Everyday Low Prices

No fake discounts or inflated MRPs. Our EDLC-EDLP model means you get the lowest possible price every day — not just during sales.

Local Sourcing

We source fresh produce from local farmers and regional suppliers, reducing transit time and supporting agriculture across India.

Community First

Each store creates 50-60 local jobs. We invest in communities through employment, fair wages, and accessible pricing for all families.

Our journey so far

2009

The Beginning

Founded our first 5,000 sq ft value retail store in C-Scheme, Jaipur — the start of a mission to make quality affordable.

2012

Rajasthan Footprint

Expanded to 10 stores across Rajasthan, proving the EDLC-EDLP model works for Indian families.

2015

Multi-State Expansion

Entered Gujarat and Maharashtra, bringing everyday low prices to new markets and millions more customers.

2018

Going Digital

Launched online ordering, extending the Laxi Mart experience to customers who prefer doorstep delivery.

2021

50 Store Milestone

Crossed 50 company-owned stores, serving millions of value-conscious families across 8 states.

2024

85+ Stores Nationwide

Operating 85+ stores across 12 states with 5,000+ employees, serving 2.5 million customers every month.
