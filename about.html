From a single store in Jaipur to 85+ stores across India

Laxi Super Mart was founded in 2009 with a simple belief — every Indian family deserves quality products at fair prices. Starting as a single 5,000 sq ft store in C-Scheme, Jaipur, we focused on what matters most: keeping costs low and passing every saving to our customers.

Unlike traditional retail, we own all our stores, work directly with manufacturers, and operate on an Everyday Low Cost — Everyday Low Price (EDLC-EDLP) model. No gimmicks, no inflated MRPs — just honest prices, every single day.

Leadership team

Founder & Managing Director

Vikram Sharma

A Jaipur native with 20+ years in retail. Vikram founded Laxi Mart with the vision of making quality groceries affordable for middle-class Indian families through the EDLC-EDLP model. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a single store to 85+ locations across India.

Chief Operating Officer

Anita Desai

Anita oversees store operations, supply chain, and vendor management across all 85+ locations — ensuring consistent quality, pricing, and customer experience at every store. She has been instrumental in building our direct-from-manufacturer procurement model.

Chief Financial Officer

Rohit Agarwal

Rohit manages financial strategy, investor relations, and the company's disciplined approach to capital allocation — keeping the EDLC model sustainable as we scale. His focus on operational efficiency has been key to maintaining margins while keeping prices low.
