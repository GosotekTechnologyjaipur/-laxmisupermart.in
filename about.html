About Laxi Super Mart

From a single neighbourhood store in Jaipur to 85+ locations across India — our story is built on freshness, affordability, and community.

Our Story

Laxi Super Mart was founded in 2002 in Jaipur, Rajasthan, with a simple but ambitious vision: to make quality groceries affordable for every Indian household.

What began as a single neighbourhood grocery store quickly earned the trust of local families through its commitment to fresh produce, fair prices, and genuine customer care. Our founder believed that no family should have to compromise on the quality of the food they put on their table.

Over two decades, that belief has fuelled our growth from one store to a nationwide network. Today, Laxi Super Mart operates 85+ stores across 12 states, serving millions of customers every month with the same neighbourhood warmth that started it all.

We partner directly with local farmers and producers to bring the freshest fruits, vegetables, dairy, and pantry staples to your doorstep — cutting out middlemen so you get better quality at lower prices.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from sourcing to shelf.

Quality First

Every product on our shelves passes rigorous quality checks. We work directly with trusted farmers and brands to guarantee freshness, purity, and safety in every item you buy.

Everyday Low Prices

By streamlining our supply chain and partnering directly with producers, we keep prices consistently low — not just during sales, but every single day of the year.

Local Sourcing

We prioritise sourcing from local farmers and artisans in every region we operate. This supports local economies, reduces carbon footprint, and ensures peak freshness.

Community Impact

From employing locally to sponsoring community events and food drives, we believe in giving back to the neighbourhoods that have made Laxi Super Mart what it is today.

Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped Laxi Super Mart over the years.

2002
Founded our first store in Jaipur, Rajasthan — a 1,200 sq ft neighbourhood grocery shop serving 200 families daily.
2008
Expanded operations into Gujarat with three new stores in Ahmedabad and Surat, establishing our first inter-state supply chain.
2014
Crossed the 100-employee milestone and launched our centralised distribution hub in Jaipur, enabling faster and fresher deliveries.
2018
Launched our online platform and mobile app, bringing the Laxi Super Mart experience to customers who prefer doorstep delivery.
2022
Reached the 50-store milestone across 8 states, with over 3,000 employees and 1.5 million monthly customers.
2025
Now operating 85+ stores across 12 states with 5,000+ employees, serving 2.5 million customers every month across India.
