Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What areas do you deliver to?
We currently deliver across 85+ locations in 12 states including Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Karnataka, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Delhi NCR, Madhya Pradesh, Punjab, and Chhattisgarh. Enter your pincode on the checkout page to confirm delivery availability in your area. We are continuously expanding to new cities and neighborhoods.
What is your return policy?
We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you receive damaged, expired, or incorrect items, you can request a return or replacement within 24 hours of delivery. Fresh produce and perishable items must be reported within 12 hours. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, RuPay debit and credit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking from all major banks, popular wallets, and cash on delivery. All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also track your order in real-time through the "My Orders" section on our website or app. Live GPS tracking is available once your delivery partner picks up your order.
Do you offer bulk/wholesale orders?
Yes, we offer bulk and wholesale pricing for businesses, restaurants, hotels, and event caterers. Contact our B2B team at wholesale@laximart.in or call +91 98765 43215 for custom quotes, recurring order schedules, and dedicated account management.
How do I apply for a job?
Visit our Careers page for current openings across technology, operations, marketing, supply chain, and store management. You can apply directly through the portal by uploading your resume. You can also send your CV to careers@laximart.in with the job title in the subject line. Our HR team typically responds within 5-7 business days.
