Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What areas do you deliver to?
Currently, Laxi Mart operates 85+ physical stores across 12 states. Home delivery is available in select cities through our online ordering platform. Visit dmart-style ordering at our website or visit your nearest store.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of purchase for non-perishable items with original receipt. Perishable items (fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat) can be returned within 24 hours if quality issues are found. Visit your store's customer service desk.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and net banking. Cash on delivery available for online orders.
How can I track my order?
For online orders, track your delivery status in your account dashboard or contact our support team with your order number. In-store purchases don't require tracking.
Do you offer bulk/wholesale orders?
Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk purchases and institutional orders (hotels, restaurants, offices, events). Contact our bulk orders team at bulk@laximart.in or call 1800-123-4568.
How do I apply for a job?
Visit our Careers page to view current openings. You can also drop your resume at any Laxi Mart store's customer service desk for store-level positions.
