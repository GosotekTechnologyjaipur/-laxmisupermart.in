Partner With Us

Become a Laxi Mart vendor — supply quality products to 85+ stores across India

Why Partner With Laxi Super Mart

Partnering with us means accessing a massive retail network, transparent business practices, and a team that values lasting relationships over short-term gains.

Direct Store Supply

All 85+ Laxi Mart stores are company-owned and operated (COCO model). As a registered vendor, you supply directly to our central warehouse and distribution centers — no sub-distributors or middlemen.

Transparent Procurement

Our procurement team evaluates products on quality, price competitiveness, and supply reliability. Payment terms are standard 30-day credit from invoice date with no hidden deductions.

Scale With Our Growth

With 10+ new stores opening annually and expanding into new states, vendor volumes grow consistently. We prioritize long-term partnerships with reliable suppliers.

Partnership Types

Choose the partnership model that best fits your business.

FMCG & Grocery Manufacturer

Supply branded or private-label grocery staples, packaged foods, beverages, home care, or personal care products to our stores.

Fresh Produce & Dairy Supplier

Supply fruits, vegetables, dairy products, eggs, or frozen foods. We require daily/weekly supply with strict cold-chain compliance and FSSAI certification.

General Merchandise & Apparel

Supply garments, footwear, bed & bath, crockery, toys, or kitchen appliances. BIS certification required where applicable.

Partnership Requirements

To ensure a smooth onboarding process, please make sure you meet the following criteria before applying.

  • Valid FSSAI license (for food products)
  • GST registration certificate
  • Minimum 2 years in business
  • Ability to supply across multiple store locations
  • Competitive pricing aligned with our EDLC model
  • Quality certifications relevant to your product category
  • Willingness to participate in periodic quality audits
