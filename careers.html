Careers at Laxi Super Mart

Join India's fastest-growing value retail chain — 85+ stores and growing

Why Work With Us

We invest in our people because they are the heart of everything we do.

Grow From the Floor Up

At Laxi Mart, 80% of our store managers started as floor associates. We believe in promoting from within and investing in every team member's growth through structured training programs.

Stability & Benefits

We offer competitive compensation, performance incentives, Provident Fund, ESI coverage, and health insurance for all full-time employees across our 85+ company-owned stores.

Make a Real Difference

Every day, our teams serve thousands of families. Whether you're a cashier, buyer, or warehouse associate — your work directly helps Indian families save on daily essentials.
